## Service Land Use
Can be a subtype of [Service Land Use]().

* Area requirements: 100-300 sqm

* [Slope](): Preferable 2-10%

* Keep close to health centers, clinics and doctors.
  
  **Close** entities get **high** score (Combine [DistanceTo]() and [Scoring]())
