## Residential Land Use
Can be a subtype of [Residential Land Use]() and influences its density.

* Area Requirement per plot: 300-500 sqm
* Floors: G+3 to G+8

## Street Blocks
Suitable [Street Block]() parameters range from

* Block-Length: 80-170 m
* Block-Width: 40-80 m
